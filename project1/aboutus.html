Commissioner's Message

The Aim of Polytechnic Education is to provide Employment Oppurtunities to every Student. Iam glad to inform you that, six thousand people got jobs this year. The courses are designed according to the requirements of the industry. Placement cells have been set up in Government Polytechnic Colleges. A centralized Placement Cell has been set up at the State Level to provide employment opportunities to the students. New syllabus has been designed to prepare students for technology 4.0. As part of Skill Development, steps are being taken to teach courses like Survey, GPS Surveying, Drone Technology, Mechatronics, Advanced CAD, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. At present 7 colleges have got NBA Recognition and other Government Polytechnics are in queue to get Recognition.
